Accessibility Features

Keyboard Navigation

Full keyboard support with visible focus indicators. Use Tab, Enter, and arrow keys to navigate.

Screen Reader Compatible

ARIA labels and semantic HTML for assistive technology. Works with JAWS, NVDA, and VoiceOver.

Color Blind Friendly

High contrast mode and distinguishable color schemes for protanopia, deuteranopia, and tritanopia.

Text-to-Speech

All text can be spoken aloud with TTS support. Multiple voice options and adjustable speech rate.

Quick Access Panel

Click the floating accessibility button on any page to quickly toggle settings without leaving your current screen.
